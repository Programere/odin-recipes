Tomato Soup

This tomato soup recipe is simple,quick, and perfect to make

when tomatoes are ripe in gardens and farmer's markets

for a delicious summertime treat.

INGRIDIENTS

  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Broth

STEPS

  1. Boil the tomatoes, onion, garlic, and broth.
  2. Run the mixture through a food mill into a large bowl.
  3. Make a roux.
  4. Add the tomato mixture and season.
