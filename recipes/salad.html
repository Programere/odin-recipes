Arugula Salad

This juicy, vibrant arugula salad features fresh cherries,

peaches, and nectarines for a summery flavor.

or an extra touch of sweetness, opt for rosé vinegar,

a rosé wine-based vinegar with a bright, fruity flavor.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar or rosé vinegar
  • 1 1/2 cups red cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 1/2 cups red cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 1/2 cups red cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 3/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1 large white peach, sliced
  • 1 cup Rainier or other yellow-flesh cherries, pitted and halved
  • 2 nectarines, sliced

STEPS

  1. Whisk together olive oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper

    in a small bowl for the dressing.

  2. Arrange tomatoes, arugula, basil, nectarines,

    and peach slices on a large platter.

    Drizzle with half the dressing.

    Top with cherries, sea salt, and remaining dressing.

    Serve immediately.
